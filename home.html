ABOUT

Australian based designer, manufacturer and exporter of unique Laser platforms

Founded on more than 30 years of research and development, Norseld has perfected the Dual Yellow laser to be the gold standard in the treatment of vascular and pigmented lesions.

Norseld operates on a worldwide basis with distributor partners in more than 30 countries. Norseld is ISO 13485:2016 certified and follows strict Australian TGA, U.S. FDA and Korea KFDA guidelines.
